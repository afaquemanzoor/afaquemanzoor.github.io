excerpt: "This study introduces 'FROBOT,' a bioinspired soft frog robot fabricated via multi-material polyjet printing and ultrathin filaments. By embedding antagonistic SMA muscle wires, the robot achieves both synchronous and asynchronous swimming modes with speeds up to 18 mm/s, demonstrating high environmental adaptability and motion consistency with biological frogs."
