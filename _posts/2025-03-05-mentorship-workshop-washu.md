Participated in a one-day intensive mentorship workshop organized by the Office of the Provost at Washington University in St. Louis.

The workshop involved case-based discussions and interactive sessions that helped shape effective mentoring approaches. Key topics included equity and inclusion, effective communication, aligning expectations, and promoting professional development.

This experience provided valuable insights into fostering supportive learning environments and strengthening mentorship practices in engineering education.
